## 📊Kabir Retail Solutions – Sales Analysis

### Project Overview

This project presents a detailed analysis of sales transactions from five Sales Representatives over a three-year period (2010–2012). The goal is to evaluate performance, identify trends, and provide actionable business insights to support decision-making for the 2013 fiscal year.

### Objectives
Identify the most consistent Sales Representative
Determine the highest sales volume generator
Analyse yearly performance trends (growth or decline)
Provide data-driven business recommendations

### 🛠️Tools Used

Git & GitHub (Version Control & Submission)

### 📈Executive Summary
Sales performance from 2010 to 2012 shows a steady increase in both quantity sold and total revenue, indicating positive business growth. Overall, the company demonstrates strong performance and is well-positioned for expansion in 2013.

💡 Business Recommendations
1. ### Efficiency
A sales representative with a high number of transactions but lower revenue indicates frequent low-value sales. This suggests the need for upselling strategies to increase transaction value.

2. ### Growth Strategy
Given the consistent increase in sales performance:
The company should consider hiring additional sales staff in 2013
Increase inventory levels to meet rising demand.

3. ### Performance Improvement
Provide training for lower-performing representatives
Implement incentive programs to reward top performers

### ✅Conclusion
The analysis shows that Kabir Retail Solutions is experiencing steady growth. With the right strategic decisions, the company can further improve sales performance and expand operations in the upcoming fiscal year.
